How Are We Different?

Unlike other non-profits, we don't save animals alone. We take help of you, meaning the savior community who helps the animals. And the donations we get directly goes to the saviors which keeps the good work going. If you wanna help animals, you can become a savior and earn a living from it.

Who Can Be Saviors?

Emergency Rescuers

If you are able to rescue animals within your city in emergency situation, you can become a savior. All you need to do is to rescue animal and bring him to one of our nearest shelter or vet.

Animal Doctors / Vets

If you can provide emergency treatment to animals, you can become a savior. All you need to do is to be available at the time of emergency no matter it's days or night.

Someone With a Vehicle

Have a vehicle? Then you can become savior too. All you need to do is to help in emergency transportation to any of our nearest vet or shelter no matter day or night.

Any Physical Space Owner

Have any physical space like a departmental store or some space in your home? Then you can become a savior by providing shelter to the animals and taking care of them.

Someone Who Can Adopt

You can become a savior by adopting a street animal and giving them a better life. One thing to keep in mind that you need to take care and regularly have to give us updates about the animal.

Someone Who Can Donate

Unable to the following tasks above? No worries you can still become a savior by donating money or clothes or food and help the animals live a better life.

Frequently Asked Question

If you are hesitating, do not worry - we are here to explain everything you might want to know.

  • Which country do you operate in?

    We're based Bangladesh but we operate worldwide. So no matter which country and city you're from, chances are there'll be our saviors in your city.

  • How do you use the donation money?

    We use the donation money to pay the saviors. Our Saviors provide your pets and stray animals medical treatment, food, clothes, shelter and more for free and in exchange of that, we use the donation money to pay them. Traditional non-profit volunteers work according to their wish and their time which isn't stable but our saviors get to work at any time you're in emergency even if it's midnight so we pay the so that they keep up the good work and help the animals better life.

  • Can you guys keep doing without dontaions?

    Most probably not. We fund the animal welfare project with the donations and ad earning recieved by showing you ads where the donations make up around 92% of the money so without your donations, we wouldn't be able to continue for long.

  • Can anyone be savior?

    Yes, if you can do any of the things mentoned above, then you can become a savior and earn enough money to make a living and you don't need any minimum age or educational qualification needed to become a savior. Even if you're 13 year old kid, you're welcome to become a savior.
